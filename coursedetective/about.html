About

CourseDetective.com allows you to search the online course directories of all UK universities, via a custom google search.

It also allows you to look for Open Educational Resources (OER), free online resources which can allow you to get a wider perspective of what study will entail.

It was created in less than two days at #oerhack - an event hosted by UKOLN on behalf of the JISC/Higher Education Academy UKOER progamme.
